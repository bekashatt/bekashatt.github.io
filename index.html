My popular projects

A closer look at a few recent collaborations and the results they delivered.

Client Georgian National Tourism Administration

The GNTA is Georgia’s official government body responsible for promoting tourism locally and internationally. Their digital platforms reach hundreds of thousands of travellers every month.

Project overview

In 2023, GNTA launched a full website redesign to improve performance and storytelling. I joined the team as a content writer, helping build the new blog section from the ground up.

My role

  • Wrote 6–8 SEO-focused blogs per month on key tourism topics and destinations.
  • Helped shape content structure and voice for both tourists and search performance.
  • Collaborated with SEO, design, and dev teams throughout content launch.

Key results

  • 80%+ of blog posts ranked in the top 3 on Google for target queries.
  • #1 rankings for competitive searches like “Travel tips for exploring Tbilisi” and “Digital nomad guide to Georgia”.
  • Average reading time: 3–5 minutes per article.
